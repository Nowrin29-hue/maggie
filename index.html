Maggi Noodles Recipe

Quick and easy step-by-step guide to making delicious Maggi noodles!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 1 ½ cups of water
  • Maggi tastemaker (comes with the packet)
  • Optional: vegetables like carrots, peas, or onions (finely chopped)
  • Optional: a pinch of salt or seasoning (if desired)

Steps to Make Maggi

  1. Boil 1 ½ cups of water in a pan or pot over medium heat.
  2. Once the water comes to a boil, add the Maggi noodles and break them into halves if preferred.
  3. Stir occasionally and let it cook for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the tastemaker (Maggi spice mix) into the boiling water and stir well to mix the flavors.
  5. If using vegetables, you can add them now and let them cook with the noodles for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes or until the noodles are soft and have absorbed the flavor.
  7. Once cooked, turn off the heat and serve hot!

Enjoy your Maggi noodles!
